Ponyo

The plot of this movie is interesting. It holds a lot of similarities to The Little Mermaid, which was on purpose. Hayao Miyazaki, said during an interview that while he did take some inspiration from the tale, it was more of an abstract inspiration rather than a retelling of it. ponyo is a perfect way to get a glimpse into a magical childhood when you're in the mood for a bright, vibrant movie to watch. Despite being overshadowed by My Neighbor Totoro, Howl's Moving Castle, Kiki's Delivery Service, and many others, it's one of their best movies. One of Ghibli's many hidden gems.

My neigbor totoro

My Neighbor Totoro is a lighthearted and enjoyable film. Similar to the other films produced by the same studio, the animation is stunning and delightful really it's flowing, joyful, and serene. Two young girls are followed throughout the film as they relocate with their family to a home populated by enchanted woodland animals that they can only see. The mood the film creates is appropriate for both a warm and windy summer afternoon and a cool and comfortable winter evening. This film is ideal for viewers of all ages because of its lovely and upbeat plot. I suggest this film to everyone who enjoys watching Studio Ghibli films or who wants to watch.

Kiki's delivery service

Kiki's Delivery Service reminds me that i have to take care of myself, trust my friends, and understand that failure is natural. Kiki's journey wasn't always an easy one. Her story was filled with struggles, sorrows, and lessons she had to learn in order to grow into a better person. When Kiki is faced with leaving her home in search of a town without a witch, she isn't scared, nor does she worry about where she might live. Instead, she takes this part of her journey in her stride and eagerly searches for a place she can make her new home.
